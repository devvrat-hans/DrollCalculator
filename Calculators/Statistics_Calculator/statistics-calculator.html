Frequently Asked Questions

What is Standard Deviation?

Standard deviation measures the dispersion of data points from their mean value, indicating how spread out the numbers are.

How to interpret Quartiles?

Quartiles divide data into four equal parts. Q1 (25th percentile), Q2 (median), and Q3 (75th percentile) help understand data distribution.
