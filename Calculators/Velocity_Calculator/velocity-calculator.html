Frequently Asked Questions

What is velocity?

Velocity is the rate of change of position of an object with respect to time, including both speed and direction. It's measured in meters per second (m/s).

How is acceleration related to velocity?

Acceleration is the rate of change of velocity with respect to time. It affects how quickly an object's velocity changes.
