Frequently Asked Questions

How is compound interest calculated?

The calculator uses the compound interest formula, considering both initial investment and regular contributions, with interest being reinvested.

What factors affect investment growth?

Investment growth is influenced by initial amount, regular contributions, interest rate, investment period, and frequency of compounding.

Why use an investment calculator?

An investment calculator helps visualize potential returns, plan saving strategies, and understand the impact of different variables on investment growth.
