Frequently Asked Questions

How are loan payments calculated?

Monthly payments are calculated using the loan amount, interest rate, and term length, considering compound interest over the loan period.

How do extra payments affect my loan?

Additional monthly payments reduce your principal faster, leading to less total interest paid and a shorter loan term.
