Frequently Asked Questions

What is Newton's Second Law?

Newton's Second Law states that force equals mass times acceleration (F = ma), describing the relationship between an object's mass, acceleration, and the force acting upon it.

What is Hooke's Law?

Hooke's Law states that the force needed to extend or compress a spring is directly proportional to the distance of extension or compression (F = kx).
