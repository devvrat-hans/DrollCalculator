Frequently Asked Questions

What is SIP?

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a method of investing a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds, typically monthly. It helps in building wealth over time through disciplined investing.

How does step-up SIP work?

Step-up SIP allows you to increase your monthly investment amount by a fixed percentage every year, helping your investments grow with your income.

Why choose SIP over lump sum?

SIP offers benefits like rupee cost averaging, disciplined investing, and the ability to start with smaller amounts compared to lump sum investments.
